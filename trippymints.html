Client Jungle Cats

01. Introduction & Challenges

O TrippyMints is an Ethereum NFT project launching with a small budget and small community. Although it didn’t necessarily have the goal of setting out, they were struggling to find any agencies that would support them and help them use their budget effectively to bring in some sales. In addition, the owner was part of a solo-team.

02. Solution

OBenco was able to take on TrippyMints, utilising their budget to 4x their Discord membership in 2 weeks. Although the collection didn’t sell out, Benco was able to bring in a good amount of sales for the project to attempt to go ahead. The owner was able to focus on the more important priorities of the project by having the Benco Team take over moderation and Social Media Management.
