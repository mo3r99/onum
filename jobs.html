What is a Marketing Manager?

Role Information: When you become a Benco Marketing Campaign Manager, you will be using Monday.com, Google Documents, and market research skills as well as our contacts to form marketing campaigns for clients using budgets provided. Your job is to provide them with a budget plan using a template with filled information and then executing the budget plan once they are happy with it. The Head of Marketing controls funds but you would get the addresses that he would send to you, you would be deciding and contacting the outlets of campaigns. To run a marketing campaign in the crypto space you must be aware of the best places to promote for NFTs, tokens etc. You must also understand what makes a good promotion and what makes a bad one. This role is highly specialised and requires past experience. We are very selective with applicants in this field.

What is Social Media Content Posting?

When you become a Benco Social Media Content Manager your role will be to look after the Twitter/Instagram/Reddit accounts of clients (depending which role you apply for). If we get a client that wants their account managed, your job will be to obtain the login and connect the account to a content scheduling software of their choosing (or your choosing if they have no preference). You're then expected to give a realistic schedule to the client of a post schedule you can do for them. 1 post per day is a realistic average for our clients. You should be able to formulate content yourself based on understanding their project concept, roadmap, timeline and more. You're expected to know what hashtags to use and the formats to use to optimise posting on the given social media you’re applying for.

What is Social Media Management?

When you become a Benco Community Manager your role is to either take up Event Manager or Collaboration Manager for our clients. As an event host you will be assigned 1 day to host events for all our clients, as a collaboration manager you will need to get 1 collaboration giveaway/advertisement/deal post per assigned server and (you will be assigned 6 servers maximum) per day and mark it on the log. Your job is to make sure the communities of our clients are active through events and interactions. You don't have to think of events and schedules - this will be done by the Heads of Department, just run them and make sure they run smoothly without issues and automatically so our clients can focus on other things. Contributing ideas and connecting with a client is important as you want to stick to their brand/style/project type.

What is Moderating?

When you become a Benco Moderator you will be assigned to 3 clients we work with, your job will be moderating their primary social medium (generally Discord or Telegram). This means interacting in chat, hyping chat up, kicking and banning spammers and people who break the rules etc. We are not expecting 24/7 non-stop work, but you are expected to cover your timezone for 5 hours to a sufficient degree. It is important you understand the crypto space and know how to research projects and answer questions.

What is Creative?

When you join the Benco Design Team you will be making graphics for clients to help them with their project in many ways. Some examples include: social media profiles, social media banners, promotional content, collaboration graphics and more. This can take the form of animations, statics, banner compositions and more. You are not expected to be a vector artist or to make graphics with no assets or palette guidance whatsoever. However, you are expected to be creative and assist clients with graphic concepts if they ask.
