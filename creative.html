Our Creative Team

The Benco Design Team offers top-level quality assets upon request. Our team consists of experts in animation, graphics design, video editing, and logo design. We can produce high-quality Instagram or Twitter posts, banners, promotional videos, animated logos, animated stickers, and much more. During onboarding, our Design Team will evaluate your current branding assets and advise where changes should be made for an optimal project image. Additionally, we will produce an animated Discord icon and Discord banner if the project does not have these already. We use a simple request form for you to request any assets you might need; whether it be a complete trailer or something as simple as a single Instagram post, we will produce an original graphic according to your request.
