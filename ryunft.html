Client Ryu

01. Introduction & Challenges

O Ryu is a dragon NFT game on the Avalanche blockchain. With unique art and a solid team the project was on a trajectory for success, however they were in need of a social setup for Discord as well as optimisation in moderating their community in order for their project to succeed, especially with the amount of new members from international alpha groups that were entering.

02. Solution

OThe Benco team introduced a team of moderators to handle the Discord chat as well as revamping the entire Discord. An event team was brought in to host regular events and interact with the community. This meant we were able to handle the mass influx of new membership.
