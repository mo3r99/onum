01. The Challenge

OGenerousRobotsDAO is a generative NFT collection on the SOLANA blockchain. With a very unique and well thought staking system and product, the project did not have enough attention to see the exponential growth required to sell it out.

02. Solution

OThe Benco Services team revamped the Discord server, began an event schedule and gamified growth with the pre-existing community to see some passive traffic increase and not make the current community disengaged. We then leveraged our Web3 marketing, influencer marketing and Twitter management to accelerate exponential growth. This was capitalised on with our strategic partnership management as it enabled contact with much bigger collections who wanted to engage with GenerousRobots.
