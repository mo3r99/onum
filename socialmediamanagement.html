Our Social Media Management Team

Benco Services' Social Media Management team all have vast experience in the space. This department covers Telegram management and Discord management. Your assigned staff will be available to assist you if you encounter any bugs or difficulties. Some key priorities of our managers include the following:

Schedule a Call
Discord Layout

The channel order and aesthetic is very important in a Discord Server. Discord is the current converter of sales in the crypto space. Embeds are important for keeping a server professional and aesthetically pleasing for the user. It also means you can pack information into channels easily without it appearing overcrowded.

Discord & Telegram Bots

Bots are needed to automate many features from moderation, announcements, connections to other media outlets, verification and more. The Benco Bot will also be added to servers to ensure maximum efficiency in our collaboration.

Cooperation

The Social Media Management team is also working on your social media with regards to the Social Media Management department. Channels and layouts will take into consideration potential events, AMAs and more.
